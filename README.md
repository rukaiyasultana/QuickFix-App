1. Introduction 
In everyday life people often need different kinds of home services such as electrical repair, 
plumbing work, appliance fixing, cleaning, and other maintenance services. In many cases it 
becomes difficult to find a reliable technician quickly, especially during urgent situations. 
People usually depend on personal contacts or nearby shops to find workers, which may take 
time and may not always guarantee good service. 
The QuickFix Android application is proposed as a solution to this problem. The main idea of 
this application is to create a simple platform where users can easily find service providers and 
request services through their smartphones. The app will help users locate technicians or 
workers based on service categories and connect them directly.

3. Problem Statement 
Finding skilled service providers is still a challenge for many people. When a problem occurs 
at home, such as an electrical issue or a plumbing leak, people often need immediate help. 
However, locating a trusted technician at the right time is not always easy. 
Most of the time people rely on recommendations from friends or local shops. This method is 
not very efficient because it takes time and there is no guarantee about the quality of service. 
In addition, customers cannot easily compare different service providers or see feedback from 
other users. 
Because of these limitations, there is a need for a mobile-based platform where customers can 
quickly search for service providers, view their details, and request services in a convenient 
way.
